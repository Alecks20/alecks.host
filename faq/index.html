Answers to any initial questions you might have.

Q: Is this sustainable?
A: No, this is a passion project, its incredibly cheap to run thanks to owning all the hardware and not colocating or renting. I do intend to keep this up for a while and would absolutely let you download server files if it shutdown.

Q: How much resources will my server get?
A: Depends on your needs, the game and amount of players but typically 4gb ram, 300% cpu (3 shared threads) and 20gb nvme storage.

Q: Where are the servers located?
A: An undisclosed location in Perth, Australia.
